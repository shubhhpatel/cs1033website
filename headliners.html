~~~~~~~~~~~ Past Rolling Loud Headliners ~~~~~~~~~~~

2015

Rolling Loud 2015, occurring during summer vacation, had an impressive lineup of headliners, including Juicy J, ScHoolBoy Q, and ASAP Ferg. Each of these artists brought their own unique style and energy to the stage, making for an unforgettable experience for the audience. Juicy J brought his signature Memphis-style rap, ScHoolBoy Q delivered his West Coast sound, and ASAP Ferg brought his Harlem swagger, making for a diverse and exciting show.

2016

The summer vacation event, Rolling Loud 2016, featured a powerhouse lineup of headliners, including Future, Young Thug, and 2 Chainz. At the time, Future was at the height of his career, having just released his album "EVOL" earlier that year. Young Thug was also making waves in the hip-hop world, with his unique style and delivery garnering him a dedicated fanbase. 2 Chainz was a well-established name in the industry, known for his catchy hooks and playful lyrics. Together, these artists brought a high-energy and unforgettable performance to Rolling Loud 2016.

2017

Rolling Loud 2017, another summer vacation hit, had a star-studded lineup of headliners, featuring Lil Wayne, Kendrick Lamar, and Future. Lil Wayne, a rap icon, had just released his album "Tha Carter IV" in 2011 and was still riding high on its success. Kendrick Lamar, who had just released his critically acclaimed album "DAMN." earlier that year, was widely regarded as one of the best rappers of his generation. Future, who had already established himself as a dominant force in hip-hop, had released his self-titled album and "Hndrxx" just a few months prior to the festival.

2018

The summer event, Rolling Loud 2018, featured a stacked lineup of headliners, including Future, J. Cole, and Travis Scott. Future, who had released his album "HNDRXX" and a self-titled album in 2017, was at the peak of his success. J. Cole, who had just released his album "KOD" earlier that year, was also on the rise, with his socially conscious lyrics and introspective style resonating with audiences. Travis Scott, who had just released his hit album "Astroworld," was quickly becoming one of the biggest names in hip-hop, known for his high-energy performances and unique sound.

2019

Held during summer vacation, Rolling Loud 2019 had a stacked lineup of headliners, featuring the Migos, Travis Scott, and Kid Cudi. The Migos, who had released their album "Culture II" in 2018, were known for their catchy hooks and infectious beats. Travis Scott, who had headlined Rolling Loud the previous year, was still riding high on the success of his album "Astroworld" and known for his wild and energetic performances. Kid Cudi, a beloved veteran of the rap game, had just released his album "Kids See Ghosts" with Kanye West and was known for his introspective and innovative style.

2021

Rolling Loud 2021, taking place during summer vacation, featured a star-studded lineup of headliners, including A$AP Rocky, Travis Scott, and Post Malone. A$AP Rocky, who had just released his album "Testing" in 2018, was known for his unique style and fashion sense as well as his lyrical prowess. Travis Scott, a Rolling Loud veteran, had released his album "Astroworld" in 2018 and was known for his high-energy performances and elaborate stage productions. Post Malone, who had released his album "Hollywood's Bleeding" in 2019, was one of the biggest names in the music industry, known for his infectious melodies and ability to blend genres.

2022

Set to take place during summer vacation, Rolling Loud 2022's headliners were some of the biggest names in the hip-hop industry, including Kanye West, Future, and Kendrick Lamar. Kanye West, who had just released his highly anticipated album "Donda," was known for his innovative style and bold artistic vision. Future, who had released his album "High Off Life" in 2020, was still a dominant force in the industry, known for his distinctive voice and memorable hooks. Kendrick Lamar, who had not released an album since "DAMN." in 2017, was widely regarded as one of the greatest rappers of his generation, with his socially conscious lyrics and complex rhyme schemes earning him critical acclaim. These headliners made Rolling Loud 2022 a highly anticipated summer music event.
